# Model attribution

Models in this directory are redistributed for the sherpa-onnx diarization engine:

## segmentation.onnx

- **pyannote segmentation-3.0**, converted to ONNX by the
  [k2-fsa/sherpa-onnx](https://github.com/k2-fsa/sherpa-onnx) project.
- Author: pyannote (Hervé Bredin et al.)
- License: [MIT](https://huggingface.co/pyannote/segmentation-3.0)
- Source: https://github.com/k2-fsa/sherpa-onnx/releases/tag/speaker-segmentation-models

- Author: [3D-Speaker / Alibaba DAMO Academy](https://github.com/modelscope/3D-Speaker)
- License: [Apache-2.0](https://github.com/modelscope/3D-Speaker/blob/main/LICENSE)
